### Project Title: The Relationship Between Sleep Patterns and Health Outcomes

### Research Question:
Do sleep duration and quality affect health indicators such as stress levels, BMI, and heart rate?

### Objectives:
- To analyze sleep duration and quality
- To examine relationships between sleep and health metrics
- To determine whether poor sleep is linked to poor health outcomes

### Introduction_
* Good sleep is a crucial part of overall health and can significantly impact individual well-being. Analyzing health and sleep data aims to explore how sleep patterns affect general health. By examining sleep quality and its relationship with health factors such as energy levels and mood, we can provide insights to help improve sleep quality and enhance overall health
## Overview
This project analyzes the relationship between sleep and health outcomes.

### Conclusion

Findings:
- There is a relationship between sleep duration and stress levels
- Poor sleep is associated with higher stress and possibly higher BMI

Limitations:
- The dataset size may be small
- Correlation does not imply causation
- Other factors (diet, exercise) are not included

Recommendation:
- Encourage healthy sleep habits
- Further research with larger datasets
